ResearchFall 2023–Present

I am working with Professor Grace Barcheck at Cornell to create and analyze a new catalog of slope failure events on Cascade volcanoes from existing seismic data. In fall 2023, I created classification models to discriminate between surface event and earthquake waveforms. I am currently exploring deep-learning pickers that can identify and classify arrivals in continuous data. The final goal is to apply my model to historic data recorded at the volcano-monitoring stations of the Pacific Northwest Seismic Network. My work in the spring semester of 2024 is funded by the Engineering Learning Initiatives program at Cornell.
https://github.com/jacksonfellows/seismoslide
